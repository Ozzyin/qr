Stunning Platform Features

Apstore gives you enterprise tools to publish, customize, and optimize your business cards.

Dynamic Redirects

Change your contact number, bio, address, or social handles at any time. The QR code stays exactly the same, bypassing printing and reprint costs.

Advanced Analytics

Real-time reports detailing exact scan frequencies, mobile device platforms (iOS, Android, Windows, Mac), referrers, and geo-locations of scanners.

Rich QR Customization

Break away from plain black QRs. Mix gorgeous radial gradients, customize dots styles, corner eyes styles, and easily embed custom center branding logos.

Direct Contact Saving

Mobile scanners don't have to copy-paste. Clicking "Save to Contacts" automatically generates a standard `.vcf` card downloading straight to their device.
